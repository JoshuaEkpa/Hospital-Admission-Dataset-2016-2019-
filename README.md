ðŸ“Š Keys Insights 
1. Age Age: Patients aged 60 - 73 years recoreded the highest number of admissions, indicating increased healthcare demand among the elderly
2. Gender Distribution: Male patients accounted for approximately 63% of the total admissions, while femmales represnted 37%.
3. Top Diagnoses: Cardiovascular and Metabolic conditions dominate admissions, including:
   i. Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)
   ii. HYpertension (HTN)
   iii. Acute Coronary Syndrome (ACS)
   iv. Diabetes Mellitus (DM)
4. Area Observation: Admission records peak in 2016, with lower values in subsequent years, suggesting possible data aggregation or reporting limitation
